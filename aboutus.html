What We’re All About

Delicious Cakes has been a top-rated bakery in Addison since 1988. When customers walk into our bakery,
they’re immediately greeted by an overwhelmingly
delicious smell of freshly baked treats that no one can resist!
We’re a family-owned bakery, and we love being a home away from home for the community.
All of our goods are handcrafted, baked, and served with a side of love. Stop by to taste them for yourself.
​ We were recently selected as one of the Top 50 Wedding Bakeries in the United States by Daily Meal.
